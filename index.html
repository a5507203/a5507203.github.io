Short Bios

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence and Carnegie Mellon University, collaborating with Prof. Tongliang Liu and Prof. Kun Zhang. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Sydney, where I was supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu and co-supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao. I received my bachelor's degree from the University of New South Wales. My research interests lie in causality and weakly supervised learning.
