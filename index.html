I am a lecturer in Machine learning at the School of Computer Science, the University of Sydney (USYD). Before joining USYD, I was a postdoc at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence and Carnegie Mellon University, collaborating with Prof. Tongliang Liu and Prof. Kun Zhang. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Sydney, where I was supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu and co-supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao. I got my bachelor's degree at the University of New South Wales. My research lie in causality and weakly supervised learning.
